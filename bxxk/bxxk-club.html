About bxxk club

Welcome to the Bxxk Club

Our goal here is simple: to read, reflect, and connect. Every month, we’ll dive into a new book and come together to share our thoughts, hot takes, favorite quotes, and maybe even a few unpopular opinions. This is a judgment-free zone, so whether you devour the book in a weekend or skim the summary the night before, your voice is always welcome here.Looking forward to great reads and even better conversations with all of you!

If analyzing a character, expanding your perspective in life by discusing with other women, sounds an ecnticing way to distress after your day job this may be for you. It is an also an opportuniy for us to be around other supporting women. Where we can let our guards down for a bit.
